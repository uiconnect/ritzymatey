Catering


We cater for outside events as big as Australia National Day (several thousand guests) and as intimate as a birthday party (several dozen guests).

If you are planning an outside event and you are thinking of oysters, sashimi, smoked salmon, prawns and other seafood delights, please call Nick Wong at Southern Rock Seafood on 1800 88 3474 or email at marketing@southernrockseafood.com to discuss your requirements. We would love to ensure that your event turns out fantastically and that your guests rave about the seafood.

Some our recent events include Australia National Day, Cloudy Bay 2015 Launch, Dutch King's Birthday and St. Patrick's Day.
