OUR TALE - OUR MISSION
X

We’re not all about fashion-we’re all about YOU.

We know who you are…

Ambitious. Relentless. Modern. Swift. Gifted. Multi -faceted. And much more…/p>

You have traveled the world and know what real sophistication looks like. You know what you want out of life and that includes your sartorial choices. How likely or easily are you to find them? Eh, not very, it seems.

Enter RitzyMatey.

We are a twenty-first century, premier fashion label set to revolutionize work wear for women around the globe.

Our multi dimensional, cross cultural ethos transcends not only to our inspirations and designs but also to our unified vision to deliver (superior service and products) to each one of you irrespective of where you are. Our one of a kind all-inclusive pricing has given a ‘law of one price’ a new meaning.

We are committed to being up to the minute with the very latestin fabric, design and style so that you don’t have to. We carefully source the choicest of silks in exclusively crafted prints from China, luxury wools from Cashmere to Merino from Italy and the finest of cottons, linens and other royal fabrics from India in a multitude of styles and patterns. Our peerless silken linings (with a dash of stretch added for your comfort!) mean the clothes not only look good but feel good from the inside out. Our high octane production team is relentless in its pursuit to give you a masterful fit each and every time.

Our unwavering commitment to bringing you only the best means providing you with head turning looks as well as a delightful and eclectic mix of merchandise to cater to your every need and preference at all times Notwithstanding, our not thrusting our very own fashion aesthetics and choices on to you season after season in collection after collection

We believe great companies are built around not just great products but great experiences. To that end, we are devoted to putting you at the center, a through a seamless shopping experience todaythoughtfully structured and tastefully designed exclusive to our brand on our e-store a superb after sales support.

So, come and be you because a woman who knows what she wants is the one who always gets it!!!
